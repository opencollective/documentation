# Launching an Organization

You can set up an Organization on Open Collective in much the same way as a regular Collective.

However, you should remember that:

* You will need to have your own bank account
* You will need your own legal entity&#x20;
* You will have to carry out all administrative tasks within your own team

* You will need your own legal entity&#x20;

{% hint style="warning" %}
If you are not sure you are able to manage your project's finances or admin effectively, you should seriously consider [applying to a Fiscal Host](../../fiscal-hosts/fiscal-hosts.md).
{% endhint %}

### Creating an Independent Organization

1. Go to [https://opencollective.com/signup/organization](https://opencollective.com/signup/organization)
2. Create your Organization by filling in the form
3. Follow the steps in your dashboard to setup all the details
