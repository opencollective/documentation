Product Overview
-----------------

## About

The internet generation needs organisations that reflect who we are; where
anybody can contribute to a shared mission; where leaders can easily change; and
where money flows in full transparency. Our product allows you creating an open
Collective for your community.

- **Creating Tiers** - Our users can create different tiers, accept one time or
  recurring donations and define different tiers for members or sponsors.

- **Setup Budget Goals** - Our users can set budget goals, say budget required
  for organzing an event and defining goals towards annual budget to enable 
	hiring for you.

- **Manage your expense online** - Using open collective you can file for expense
  or invoice online, can comment, approve/reject and keep picture of the receipt
	and invoice private.

- **Transparent Ledger** - The transactions enabled in and out are public
  automatic pdf and invoice can be generated.

- **Open Data and Open Source** - We are open source. The user can export list
  of all transactions, export the list of all your backers with their email
	address at any time.

- **Manage Multiple Collectives** - Open Collectives makes it easy for hosting
  multiple collectives on single legal entity. We send you monthly email report
	with all transactions per caggregated in a CSV and a PDF document with all
	invoices and expenses that have been paid. This makes the work of your
	accountant a breeze.

- **Events**  - You can create events.

## Features

### Collectives

A page to tell the world about your project and engage supporters.

### Raise Money

Accept donations and sponsorship.

### Pay Expense

Reimburse expenses and pay invoices.

### Transparent Budget

Automatic financial reporting and accountability.

### Tiers

Levels or rewards for backers and sponsors.

### Goals

Funding milestones you're reaching for.

### Events

Tickets sales go straight to your Collective budget

### Organisations

Sponsor Collectives on behalf of a company.

### Backer Updates

Keep your supporters in the loop about your achievements and news.

### Fiscal Hosting

Create an umbrella entity to support a community of Collectives.

## Profiles

Tell the world who you are and show off the Collectives you're managing or
supporting.
