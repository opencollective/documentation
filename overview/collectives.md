Collectives Overview
---------------------

## About

Collectives allows you to create a transparent funding page for your project in
just a few clicks. No need to incorporate, hire a lawyer and accountant, or define
organisations roles.

## Features

- **CRUD Operations** - You can create, delete, update a collective.
- **Fiscal Hosts** - For raising or spending money you'll need a Fiscal Host.
  There are different host that serve different missions, interest areas and
	geographics. You can find all the fiscal hosts [here](https://opencollective.com/hosts).
- **Tiers** - You can define different tiers for your collective per backers and
  sponsors.
- **Events** - You can set up events for your community and sell tickets that go straight to your Collective budget.
- **Funding Options** - We encourage collectives to get creative about funding
  channels beyond the standard reward tiers. You can have ethical advertising, 
	swap shop, affiliations, VIP support. sponsored Bounties, voting rightts.
