# Relatedcollectives

{% hint style="info" %}
Creating relatedcollectives is [a manual process](../internal/queries/relatedcollective-queries.md) at the moment. If you want to do it, please send an email to [support@opencollective.com](mailto:support@opencollective.com) describing what you'd like.
{% endhint %}

Relatedcollectives are a way to display the different collectives or local projects that came out of your main initiative. Collectives are not strongly tied together:

* They **don't** share the same budget
* They can have different hosts, with different currencies
* Admins of one Collective are **not** automatically admin of the other

On the Collective page, relatedcollectives will be displayed under `More ways to contribute`
